About Me

Hello! I'm Saloni Sharma, a passionate and self-motivated aspiring data analyst with a background in commerce and a deep interest in data-driven decision-making. I hold a B.Com in Computers from RKDF University, and I’ve combined my business knowledge with strong technical skills in tools like SQL, Excel, Python, Power BI, and AI-powered productivity tools to solve real-world problems.

I started my journey by understanding business fundamentals, and then transitioned into the world of data analytics by working on hands-on projects in domains like e-commerce, AI job markets, sales forecasting, and inventory analysis. Each project taught me how to extract meaning from data, find hidden insights, and present them in a way that helps others make informed decisions.

What makes me stand out is my ability to blend business logic with technical analysis. From cleaning messy datasets and writing optimized SQL queries to designing impactful Power BI dashboards, I approach every task with curiosity and a commitment to clarity.

Beyond technical tools, I’ve also explored how AI tools and automation can speed up workflows, increase accuracy, and boost productivity. Whether it’s generating code, summarizing data insights, or automating repetitive analysis steps — I believe in using modern tools to work smarter, not harder.

Some of my most rewarding projects include:

  • Google Shopping Analysis: Scraped real-time e-commerce data and used Python, SQL, and Power BI to uncover patterns in pricing, reviews, and trust signals.
  • AI Job Market Insights: Performed deep SQL-based analysis on job postings to understand trends in roles, skills, remote work, and salaries.
  • Zepto Inventory & Superstore Sales Dashboards: Created interactive Power BI dashboards to help with stock optimization and sales performance monitoring.

I'm constantly learning and evolving. I stay updated with the latest in data tools, participate in online challenges, and connect with the data community to sharpen my problem-solving skills.

Looking ahead, I’m actively seeking opportunities where I can apply my data skills in a collaborative environment, contribute to impactful projects, and grow as a professional. My goal is not just to work with data — but to turn data into stories, strategies, and solutions.

Let’s connect, collaborate, and make data work for business impact!
