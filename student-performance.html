Student Habit & Performance Dashboard: Visualizing the Link Between Discipline and Academic Success

🧠 About the Topic:
Students' academic outcomes are influenced not just by their intelligence but by daily habits—such as sleep patterns, screen time, and study hours. This project explores how different student behaviors impact performance using an interactive Power BI dashboard.

🎯 Objective:
To analyze student habits and their correlation with academic performance by building a visually appealing, data-driven dashboard that can help educators, parents, and students identify key behavioral factors influencing results.

⚙️ Process:

Data Modeling:

  • Cleaned and structured raw data into meaningful categories (habit hours, performance metrics, subject scores).

Dashboard Creation in Power BI:

  • Designed multiple interactive visuals including:
    • Student-wise performance breakdown
    • Habit comparison by subject and gender
    • Correlation between study hours, screen time, sleep, and grades

KPI Integration:

  • Added slicers, filters, and drill-throughs to allow users to analyze by individual, class, or habit category.

📌 Key Insights:

  • 📖 Study Time Matters: Students who consistently studied 3+ hours daily performed significantly better.
  • 📱 Screen Time vs Grades: Excessive screen time showed a negative correlation with overall academic scores.
  • 🛌 Sleep Balance: Students getting 7–8 hours of sleep had better focus and grades.
  • 🚻 Gender Patterns: Female students showed slightly better performance consistency across all subjects.
  • 📐 Subject Trends: Math scores were most sensitive to changes in study habits.

🧾 Conclusion:

This dashboard brings data storytelling to life by turning student behavior data into actionable academic insights. It showcases strong Power BI skills, including DAX, visualization design, and interactivity features—perfect for educators and institutions seeking to foster better learning habits. This project reflects my capability to convert raw data into decisions that matter.
